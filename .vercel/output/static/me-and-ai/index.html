01

The philosophy

Most people prompt a chatbot. I orchestrate agents to handle complex, multi-step tasks. The difference is not the tool — it is the environment you design around it.

8 Project Intelligence Principles

01 Shared memory

AI needs to understand the project, why it matters, and what must not be misunderstood.

02 Current orientation

A visible sense of where things stand: agreed, open, next.

03 Evidence discipline

Ideas grounded in evidence, not assumptions or storytelling.

04 Asset awareness

Both humans and AI know what materials exist.

05 Collaboration clarity

How to work together well, not just what the project is about.

06 Decision continuity

Decisions preserved so the team does not restart the same thinking.

07 Role clarity

Every collaborator — human or AI — has a clear role.

08 Reusable patterns

Workflows that work are saved, refined, and reused.

02

The method

From idea to live site in one conversation. I set up project intelligence structures — CLAUDE.md files, agent roles, shared memory, and reusable workflows — that turn a chatbot conversation into a coordinated system of agents.

1
Describe the vision

Be specific about what you want, how it should feel, who it is for.

2
Set up the environment

CLAUDE.md, agent roles, memory files, permissions. This is the project intelligence layer.

3
Orchestrate and build

AI reads, plans, implements, tests. You guide with feedback and judgment.

4
Ship

Commit, push, deploy. Live in minutes, not weeks.
